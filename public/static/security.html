Security Center

Learn how we protect your accounts and what you can do to stay safe.

Suspect fraud or unauthorized activity?
Call us immediately at 1-800-MERIDIAN or lock your card in the mobile app.

How we protect you

256-bit SSL encryption on all transactions, real-time fraud monitoring, multi-factor authentication, and automatic session timeouts. Your accounts are watched 24/7.

Zero liability protection

You're never responsible for unauthorized transactions on your Meridian accounts when reported promptly. We'll reimburse you and investigate immediately.

Fraud alerts

Set up real-time alerts for transactions, login attempts, and balance changes. Get notified by email, text, or push notification — your choice.

Card lock/unlock

Misplaced your card? Lock it instantly from the app. Found it? Unlock just as fast. No need to call or wait for a replacement.

Protect yourself from phishing

Meridian will never ask for your password, PIN, or full SSN by email or phone. If something seems suspicious, don't click — call us directly.

Identity theft resources

If you believe your identity has been compromised, we'll help you place fraud alerts, freeze your credit, and file reports with the FTC.
