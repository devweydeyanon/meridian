Common questions

What do I need to open a checking account?
You'll need a valid government-issued ID, your Social Security number, and an initial deposit (amount varies by account type). You can open most accounts online in minutes.
Can I switch my checking account type later?
Yes. You can upgrade or change your checking account type at any time by visiting a branch or calling us. Your account number stays the same.
How do I set up direct deposit?
Share your Meridian routing number and account number with your employer's payroll department. You can find both in the mobile app or online banking.
Is there a minimum balance to open an account?
Most checking accounts can be opened with any amount. There is no minimum balance requirement to keep your account open.

Need help choosing?

Call 1-800-MERIDIAN or visit a branch.
